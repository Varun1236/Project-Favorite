About

My hobby is making 3d shapes out of paper. So far I have made 7 shapes: Cube, Hexagonal Prism, Triangular Prism, Octahedron, Pentagonal Pyramid, Dodecahedron, and Icosahedron.

1. Draw the template of the 3d shape you want to make.

2. Add flaps to the necessary places.

3. Cut it out, don't cut of the flaps!

4. Glue the flaps to the sides of the shapes.
5. It's done!

Favorite 3D Shapes

My Favorite 3D shapes are the Icosahedron and Dodecahedron because they are very challenging and fun to make.
